About

The goal of Total Tax Burden is to make make taxes imposed by government more transparent and to show the impact on the citizens who pay them. Government does a good job of hiding and obfuscating taxes and we want to help level the playing field. We hope to play a part in helping citizens make informed decisions.

We're not affiliated with any political party or organization.
